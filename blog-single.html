Measuring Temperature using LM35 Module

Internet of ThingsDecember 15, 2023

My first project in the field of IoT was "Measuring Temperature Using the LM35 Module", which was assigned by my physics lecturer in the first semester. This project was my initial step in understanding sensor concepts and microcontrollers in the world of the Internet of Things.

At that time, I had no knowledge of microcontrollers, hardware programming, or IoT systems in general. All terms like ESP32, analog sensors, and data communication were still unfamiliar to me.

In this project, we used ESP32 as the main microcontroller due to its flexibility in connectivity. The LM35 sensor was used to measure the ambient temperature, while the Blynk application displayed the temperature data in real-time on mobile devices.

watch here

Although it seemed simple, completing this project took quite a lot of time. We had to understand the basics of programming, connect the components correctly, and troubleshoot various technical issues that arose during the process. We even had to replace components frequently due to damage caused by incorrect wiring, which taught us to be more careful when handling hardware. However, this experience was a valuable first step in my journey into the world of IoT.
